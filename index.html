I'm not quite certain which aspects of computer science I enjoy most, so I'm dabbling in a variety of different things until I have a better idea.

When I'm not debugging my code, I enjoy playing basketball/volleyball, talking with friends, reading, playing tetris, backing KickStarter projects, searching YouTube for cover artists and power napping.
